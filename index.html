What Is Donor Relations and Stewardship?

Donor relations and stewardship are the practices that help an organization say thank you, communicate impact, and build ongoing relationships with donors. Stewardship is more than a single letter or event. It is a cycle of gratitude, transparency, and engagement that encourages donors to feel connected to the mission over time.

Donor relations work often includes writing acknowledgment letters, organizing events, reporting on the impact of gifts, and making sure donors feel seen, appreciated, and informed. Strong stewardship can lead to stronger trust, continued support, and a more vibrant community of donors.

Why Data Matters in Stewardship

Behind every letter, invitation, or event is a set of donor records that provides context. Accurate data supports personalization, timing, and meaningful communications.

Advancement services and stewardship teams work together to keep donor information organized and reliable. Strong data leads to clearer messaging and stronger donor experiences.

Examples of Useful Donor Data

  • Contact Information: Mailing address, email, phone.
  • Gift History: Dates, amounts, and designations.
  • Preferences: Communication style and interests.
  • Engagement Notes: Meeting summaries or event attendance.

Follow-Up After Gifts and Events

After each interaction or event, recording notes helps guide the donor's next step with the institution. These details build a long-term picture of the donor's journey.
